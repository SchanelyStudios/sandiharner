Myers-Briggs Consulting

Helping your employees collaborate to succeed

As a certified consultant for the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI), I am qualified to assess your group's diverse personalities and teach you and them how to understand themselves and others. This method has a long, diverse, and successful history of helping people thrive together. These skills enable employees to make constructive use of their individual differences and to be more productive as they learn to approach problems in healthy ways. Participants will learn how to leverage their own natural strengths, improve teamwork, and increase communication effectiveness.

For maximum benefit, two four-hour workshops are recommended.
